About Me


I have switched my major two times during my time in college.

I started off as a creative writing major since I wrote *cough* fanfiction *cough* back in the day that people really liked.
The idea of being an author seemed worthwhile at the time but I realized it wasn't going to pan out well for a career choice.
I then switched into IT (Information Technology) because I had an interest in computers and I thought I could make a living off
of helping people fix both theirs and mine.
Needless to say, I was downright miserable for the next two years because of that decision.
However, at the same time, I discovered that I liked coding.
It's just that I was robbing myself of my creativity, the quality I pride myself on.

When I transferred to the University of Central Florida, it was during the first semester that
I came to the decision that I couldn't do IT anymore.
I took a risk and, thankfully, because of how similar Digital Media was to IT, I was able to switch into the program.
I would be starting the very next semester...after a tragedy occured in my family.
I won't go into the specifics, but I lost someone that I wish I still had in my life today.
So, with that said, my personal life may be a bit pessimistic right now.
Its not all bad but its been really hard for me to look on the bright side of things.
However, I'm glad that I'm a Digital Media major.
I wouldn't have it any other way.
