Procrastination Puzzle Pie

Expected Release Date: March 2024

Do you remember that one Gameboy Advance game where you have to input commands as this circle keeps circling the border of the pie and fills it with pieces over time until you complete it? No? Just me?


Well, guess what? I made it into a to-do list application!


Kiss procrastination goodbye with this revived concept made to gamify your mundane life. All you have to do is set up a pie with a name, category and deadline and you're off to the races. All you gotta do then is complete your tasks by your set deadline or watch as all of the gems fall out one by one when you wake up each day, wondering where all the time went. If I had to give out a reccommendation, however, I think completing the tasks would be a lot more fun for you.


This application will be available to anyone who has access to the Google Play Store.
